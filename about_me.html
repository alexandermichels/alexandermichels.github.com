Hello, I'm Alexander Michels. I'm currently an undergrad at Westminster College in New Wilmington, PA. At Westminster I am an Honors Math and Financial Economics double major with a Computer Science minor and currently my focus is my Honors Research and preparing for graduate school applications. I am hoping that I'll be able to continue pursuing my passions for Computer Science, Economics, and Mathematics in graduate school as I am really enjoying learning more about algebra, computational complexity, logic, mathematical economics, and topology in my upper-level classes, research, and independent studies.

When I'm not at Westminster or pursuing research on my freetime, I love spending time developing software for autonomous robotics, partaking in the Financial Analyst program with Moody Investor Service's Ben Nelson, teaching and tutoring computer science and mathematics, and working with the Westminster Endowment Fund. I also love movies (especially horror), music (Can't Swim, Foxing, Have Mercy, Hot Mulligan...), and television.
